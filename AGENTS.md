# Marketing Swarm Operating Rules

## Global rules
- Output must be ready to paste into real work.
- Prefer short, structured deliverables: options + final recommendation.
- No vague filler. No repetition of what is already visible in the KV or brief.
- Keep tone brand-safe and family-friendly when the audience includes kids/parents.

## Output format standards
- Always include a "Final" section.
- When generating options, cap at 8–12 strong options, not 50 weak ones.
- When uncertain, state assumptions explicitly.

## Review guidelines
- Flag factual claims that require verification.
- Flag compliance risks (pricing, eligibility, misleading promo language).
- Highlight any copy that could be interpreted as a guarantee.
